# Lost love story
---

## Genre

The Genre of this story is **Romance** + **Drama**.

## Theme

Sometimes we loose people we love because we are not ready for them.

## Main character

**Name**: Ken

**Age**: 26

**Character traits**:

- Caring
- Loving
- Emotional
- Ambitious
- Hardworking
- Intelligent
- Overtinks
- Panics under emotional pressure

## Core idea

He found her when he was not ready, and lost her when he needed her most.

## How they met

They first met at an attachment job in Ruiru, Kiambu county. It was love at first sight for Ken and to some extent for her. From the moment he first saw her, something felt different. He couldn't explain it, but he knew she was special.

Ken was the first to fall for her. He was drawn to her presence, energy, and the way she carried herself.
