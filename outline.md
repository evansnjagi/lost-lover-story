# Lost love story
---

## Genre

The Genre of this story is **Romance** + **Drama**.

## Theme

Sometimes we loose people we love because we are not ready for them.

## Main character

**Name**: Daev

**Age**: 26

**Character traits**:

- Caring
- Loving
- Emotional
- Ambitious
- Hardworking
- Intelligent
- Overtinks
- Panics under emotional pressure

## Core idea

He found her when he was not ready, and lost her when he needed her most.

## How they met

They first met at an attachment job in Ruiru, Kiambu county. It was love at first sight for Daev and to some extent for her. From the moment he first saw her, something felt different. He couldn't explain it, but he knew she was special.

Daev was the first to fall for her. He was drawn to her presence, energy, and the way she carried herself. Although who knows, maybe she also found him attractive at first sight.

## The girl

**Name**: Evda

**Personality**:

- Caring
- Honest
- Vocal
- Spontaneous
- Interactive
- Sweet
- Hardworking

## What made her special to Daev

- Her joyful face that naturally drew people in.
- Her playful nature that made every moment feal alive.
- Her strong sense of self-respect.
- Her passion and dedication to work.
- Her soft laugh that alleviated stress and brough him peace.

## Daev's biggest weakness in love

- He cared too much.
- He loved too deeply.
- He was highly emotional.
- He got attached too quickly and beyond healty limits.
- His honesty, sometimes without emotional control, hurt the relationship.
